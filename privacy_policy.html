Privacy Policy (English Version)

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Flinging App ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Profile information (name, age, gender, bio)
  • Profile photos
  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Location data (for finding nearby matches)

3. Camera Permission

We request camera access to take profile photos, capture photos for facial verification during signup, and update your profile pictures. Photos are stored securely on our servers and not shared with third parties without your consent.

4. Location Permission

We request location access to find nearby potential matches and improve matching algorithms. Background location access is used to update your location for better match suggestions and can be disabled in your device settings.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, delete, or update your personal data at any time.

6. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: flingings0@gmail.com
